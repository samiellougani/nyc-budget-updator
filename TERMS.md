# SMS Terms & Conditions — NYC Fiscal Policy Digest

**Program name:** NYC Fiscal Policy Digest (nyc-budget-updator)

**Program description:** A personal, automated weekly text-message digest of
New York City and New York State fiscal policy news (budgets, tax proposals,
and related legislation), sent to a small list of individuals who have
personally asked the program operator to receive it.

**Message frequency:** Up to 1 recurring automated message per week.

**Message and data rates may apply.** Charges depend on your mobile carrier
and plan.

**Opt-out:** You can cancel at any time. Text **STOP** to unsubscribe, or ask
the program operator directly to be removed. After you send STOP, you will
receive no further messages from this program.

**Help:** Text **HELP** for help, contact the program operator directly, or
open an issue at
<https://github.com/samiellougani/nyc-budget-updator/issues>.

**Support contact:** Sami Ellougani — sami.ellougani1@gmail.com

**Carriers** (e.g., AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon) **are not liable for delayed or
undelivered messages.**
